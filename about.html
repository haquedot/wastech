About The Wastech Summit 2026

Wastech Summit 2026 is a leading platform for advancing waste management, recycling, and circular economy initiatives across the region. Set in Dubai this April, the summit convenes policymakers, technology experts, sustainability leaders, investors, and practitioners to discuss strategies and showcase practical solutions redefining waste management.


Focusing on real progress toward a circular and zero-waste future, Wastech 2026 highlights developments such as automated recycling systems, innovative waste-to-value models, and collaborative frameworks for sustainable growth. The event delivers actionable knowledge, encourages cross-sector partnerships, and supports measurable outcomes for a cleaner, smarter, and more resilient world.

Our Mission

To accelerate progress in waste management and sustainability in the MENA region by creating the most effective platform for deal-making, knowledge transfer, and collaborative action.
